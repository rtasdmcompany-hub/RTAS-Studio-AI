**Classification:** Sales / GTM

## ICP summary

**Best-fit customer:** An English-speaking creator, agency, or marketing company that needs recurring short-to-mid-form video (ads, social, music video, animation concepts), will pay for predictable monthly seconds, and cares about brand-safe / authorized-use positioning.

**Disqualify quickly:** Buyers seeking unauthorized likeness tools, free unlimited generation, or mature enterprise SSO/procurement on day one without a pilot mindset.

## Cross-persona pain points (shared)

1. **Cost and latency of traditional video** for always-on digital channels  
2. **Unclear ROI** of experimental AI tools without second-based budgeting  
3. **Trust / brand-safety** anxiety around generative likeness and misuse  
4. **Tool fragmentation** across research demos, consumer apps, and avatar platforms  
5. **Procurement friction** when vendors lack MoR, refund policy, or published legal pages  

## Cross-persona buying motivations (shared)

1. Faster concept-to-cut cycles  
2. Predictable monthly seconds (2000s Standard/Premium)  
3. Low-risk trial (Tester $5)  
4. Commercial use-case fit (ads, music video, animation)—not only novelty clips  
5. Compliance-friendly story for clients and payment partners
