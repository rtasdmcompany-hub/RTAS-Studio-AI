## 1. What it is

**Verified fact:** RTAS Studio AI is an AI video studio web product operated by RTAS Digital Marketing Company (operating from Pakistan), available at https://rtasstudio.com.

It enables users to generate video from text, images, lyrics, voice, and related inputs — including workflows for music videos, commercials / marketing video, animation, social content, original AI characters, and talking avatars with lip sync on **authorized** media — plus **Identity Preservation** (identity consistency) for likenesses the user owns or is authorized to use.

**Pricing (verified fact):**

| Premium | USD $249 / month | 2,000 credits / billing period | 4K cinematic masters; priority Identity Preservation queuing |

## 4. Who it is NOT for

Be explicit — this protects brand trust and reduces support burden.

| Anti-persona | Reason |
|--------------|--------|
| Users seeking to **impersonate** real people without rights | Violates Trust & Safety / AI Policy; Identity Preservation is authorized-only |
| Buyers expecting **on-prem / air-gapped** frontier models today | Current product is cloud SaaS with third-party model providers |
| Teams needing **full NLE replacement** (frame-accurate pro editorial) | Studio AI generates and stitches; it is not Adobe Premiere / Avid |
| Procurement teams requiring **SOC 2 Type II + MSA + SSO on day one** | Enterprise packaging is roadmap; not a verified current offering |
| Users wanting **unlimited free commercial output** | Tester is evaluation; commercial licensing is for active Standard/Premium under Terms |
| Anyone expecting **guaranteed same-day 4K at unlimited concurrency** | Output depends on GPU load, tier, and provider capacity (Terms disclose this) |

## 6. Segment deep-dives

### 6.1 Business customers (SMBs & growth brands)

- Need: faster creative cycles than agency-only production  
- Message: credit transparency + commercial Terms on paid plans  
- Risk to acknowledge: brand safety depends on user inputs and policy compliance  

### 6.2 Creators

- Need: volume of shorts / music visuals without crew  
- Message: Tester path; Identity Preservation for *their* authorized likeness  
- Anti-message: do not market deepfake impersonation  

### 6.3 Agencies

- Need: client turnaround, reusable workflows, clear commercial rights  
- Message: Standard as workhorse; Premium for hero cinematic  
- Gap (honest): multi-seat / client workspaces — treat as future packaging unless shipped  

### 6.4 Marketing companies

- Need: always-on content calendar fill  
- Message: seconds budgeting (2,000s/mo) maps to campaign planning  
- Estimate: monthly spend often aligns to one Standard seat or small seat cluster (see `../sales/ICP.md`)  

### 6.5 Media houses

- Need: pre-viz, variant generation, experimental formats  
- Positioning: acceleration layer beside traditional craft — not replacement of craft unions/crews overnight  

### 6.6 Studios (creative / production)

- Need: look-dev and pitch materials  
- Gap: on-set / DI integration not claimed  

### 6.7 Enterprise

- Current honesty: early-stage SaaS with strong legal foundation; **not** claiming Fortune deployment count  
- Required before enterprise-ready claims: SSO, DPA packaging, uptime SLA, security questionnaire pack, named account management
