# Unique Selling Proposition (USP)

**Document type:** Positioning / brand  
**Last updated:** 22 July 2026  

---

## Primary USP (one sentence)

**RTAS Studio AI is the AI video studio that combines multi-format professional generation (music videos, commercials, animation, and more) with authorized Identity Preservation and transparent second-based credits — backed by a live legal policy suite and Merchant-of-Record billing.**

---

## USP pillars

| Pillar | Proof available today | Not a claim |
|--------|----------------------|-------------|
| **Studio, not a toy** | Category coverage in product/legal descriptions | “Best video quality in the world” |
| **Authorized identity** | Identity Preservation + Trust & Safety / AI Policy constraints | Deepfake-for-hire |
| **Honest metering** | 1 credit = 1 second; published Tester/Standard/Premium | Hidden overage tricks |
| **Commercial path** | Standard/Premium commercial licensing under Terms | Tester = commercial free-for-all |
| **Legal maturity** | Live terms/privacy/refund/cookies/ai-policy/trust-safety v1.1 | Instant enterprise SOC certification |
| **Global sell motion** | Domain + Paddle MoR architecture | MoR review necessarily complete |

## Elevator versions

### 10 seconds
AI video studio for creators and marketers — credits by the second, authorized identity consistency, clear plans.

### 30 seconds
RTAS Studio AI helps creators, agencies, and marketing teams generate music videos, commercials, and animation with transparent credits (1 credit = 1 second). Start with a $5 Tester, scale to Standard HD or Premium 4K. Identity Preservation is for authorized likenesses only, with production legal policies live at rtasstudio.com. Billing via Paddle as Merchant of Record.

### Investor version (honest)
Early-stage AI video studio SaaS from RTAS Digital Marketing Company (Pakistan). Product engineering freeze referenced at v1.0.0; Phase 11 builds the business/investor documentation layer. Differentiates on workflow packaging, authorized identity, credit economics, and legal/MoR readiness rather than claiming proprietary frontier model ownership or undisclosed traction.

## Competitive contrast (fair)

| Buyer question | Typical category answer | RTAS answer |
|----------------|-------------------------|-------------|
| How do I pay for usage? | Seats, weird tokens, or opaque generations | Seconds as credits; published prices |
| Can I keep a face consistent? | Sometimes; rights unclear | Identity Preservation **authorized-only** |
| Can I use output commercially? | Varies | Standard/Premium under Terms; Tester = evaluation |
| Who is the seller for tax? | Often the startup | Paddle MoR (policy-stated) |
| Is this just avatars? | Synthesia/HeyGen-class focus | Broader studio modes including music/commercial/animation |

## Anti-USP (what we refuse to sell)

- Unauthorized impersonation  
- Guaranteed identical output to any named competitor demo  
- Fabricated ARR/users for fundraising  
- “Unlimited 4K with no fair use / capacity constraints”
