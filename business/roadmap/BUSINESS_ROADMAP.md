## 2026 — Foundation & first commercial traction

**Theme:** Make the commercial engine real: reliable checkout, repeatable self-serve conversion, agency-aware messaging, and measurable retention signals—without inventing vanity metrics in public materials.

### Milestones (planning)

| Workstream | 2026 milestone (PLANNING ASSUMPTION) |
|------------|--------------------------------------|
| **Revenue** | Establish consistent MoR collection; pursue **Base scenario** of early SaaS run-rate compatible with Standard/Premium mix (illustrative annualized band **$50k–$400k** across Low–High—not a forecast) |
| **Users** | Grow paying accounts via Tester→Standard ladder; prioritize quality of fit over raw signups (illustrative paying-account band **low hundreds to low thousands** by YE2026 across scenarios) |
| **Enterprise** | Run **design-partner / pilot** conversations; ship sales one-pagers from this business pack; do **not** claim enterprise installed base |
| **Funding** | Operate primarily on bootstrapped / operator capital; optional **angel / pre-seed exploration** only if MoR + retention evidence exists (funding is optional, not assumed) |
| **Global expansion** | English-first global web; deepen outreach in South Asia, Middle East, and diaspora creator/agency networks |

### 2026 scenarios (revenue — PLANNING ASSUMPTION only)

| Scenario | Illustrative annualized revenue band | Narrative |
|----------|--------------------------------------|-----------|
| Low | ~$50k–$120k | Slow MoR; weak conversion; quality/support drag |
| Base | ~$120k–$250k | Steady self-serve + light agency outbound |
| High | ~$250k–$400k+ | Strong agency adoption + Premium mix |

### 2026 success criteria (operational)

- Checkout path production-stable under Paddle  
- Support SLAs credible for paid tiers (support@rtasstudio.com)  
- Positioning and ICP docs used in all external pitches  
- Zero tolerance for deepfake/face-swap marketing drift

## 2027 — Agency scale & product depth

**Theme:** Convert early traction into a repeatable agency/marketing-company motion; deepen workflow/API where evidence demands; raise brand in competitive set without fake leadership claims.

### Milestones (planning)

| Workstream | 2027 milestone (PLANNING ASSUMPTION) |
|------------|--------------------------------------|
| **Revenue** | Illustrative annualized band **$400k–$2.0M** across Low–High; Base oriented to **~$0.7M–$1.2M** if 2026 Base+ achieved |
| **Users** | Multi-seat agency accounts become meaningful share of revenue; Creator long-tail remains volume engine |
| **Enterprise** | Formal pilot program (3–10 design partners as a planning count); security/FAQ pack; commercial terms experimentation |
| **Funding** | If metrics support: evaluate **seed** process; if not: remain efficient and profitable-lean. Funding remains a choice, not a destiny. |
| **Global expansion** | Localized campaigns (not necessarily full localization) for priority regions; partner/affiliate experiments |

### 2027 scenarios (PLANNING ASSUMPTION)

| Scenario | Illustrative ARR-like band | What must be true |
|----------|----------------------------|-------------------|
| Low | ~$400k–$700k | Flat agency motion; heavy churn |
| Base | ~$700k–$1.2M | Agency playbook works; Premium mix healthy |
| High | ~$1.2M–$2.0M+ | Viral creator loop + agency land-and-expand |

1. **Generation quality & reliability** — Table stakes vs. Runway/Pika/Kling/Luma/Veo perception  
2. **Trust & compliance** — Maintain AUP leadership in messaging and product policy  
3. **Agency enablement** — Case studies (real only), seat admin, billing clarity  
4. **Workflow depth** — Automation and API as evidence-based roadmap, not vanity  
5. **Brand** — Distinct RTAS Studio AI creative identity (see reserved `branding/`)

## Funding logic (non-binding)

| Stage | When it makes sense (assumption) | When it does not |
|-------|----------------------------------|------------------|
| Angel / pre-seed | MoR live, early paid retention, clear ICP | Before checkout works |
| Seed | Agency playbook repeatable, quality competitive for price | To “buy” vanity users |
| Series A | Efficient growth + enterprise wedge evidence | To paper over weak retention |

RTAS may remain **operator-funded** longer if capital markets or terms are unfavorable. Independence is a valid path.
