## What it is

RTAS Studio AI is an **AI video studio**: a commercial product for generating video from text and images, oriented toward **commercials, music videos, animation**, and brand storytelling—with **Identity Preservation** available for **authorized** content workflows.

It is packaged as a SaaS subscription with a **seconds-based credit model** (1 credit = 1 second) and three published tiers:

| Tier | Price | Credits / access | Role in positioning |
|------|-------|------------------|---------------------|
| Tester | $5 | 30 seconds · 5 days | Prove value with minimal commitment |
| Standard | $89 / month | 2000 seconds | Core studio for creators and teams |
| Premium 4K | $249 / month | 2000 seconds | Higher cinematic / 4K quality positioning |

Checkout is processed via **Paddle** as Merchant of Record (subject to domain approval). Trust artifacts are live: Terms, Privacy, Refund, Cookies, AI Policy, Trust & Safety v1.1.

## What it is for

- Producing marketing and campaign video without a full traditional crew for every asset  
- Exploring creative directions (concepts, animatics, finished social/ad cuts) faster than classic production calendars  
- Augmenting agency and studio pipelines with AI-generated shots and sequences  
- Authorized brand/talent continuity via Identity Preservation (permissioned use)  
- Teams that need **predictable spend** (monthly seconds budget) rather than opaque enterprise quotes alone  

## What it is NOT for

| Not for | Why |
|---------|-----|
| Face-swap, deepfake, or unauthorized likeness abuse | Product marketing and AUP posture prohibit this; Identity Preservation is for authorized content only |
| Claiming to replace every Hollywood VFX pipeline on day one | Early-stage studio product vs. large-cap research platforms |
| Pure talking-head avatar L&D / corporate presenter use cases as the primary category | That category is owned by specialized avatar vendors (e.g., Synthesia, HeyGen); RTAS is studio/generation–led |
| Buyers seeking free unlimited generation | Economics are credit-metered and paid |
| Illegal, non-consensual, or policy-violating content | Trust & Safety and Terms govern use |

## Primary use cases

1. **Commercials & performance ads** — Short promotional spots for products and services  
2. **Social & campaign creatives** — High-volume variants for paid social and organic channels  
3. **Music videos & creative films** — Stylized motion for artists and creative directors  
4. **Animation & motion storytelling** — Animated sequences and concept visualization  
5. **Brand world-building** — Mood films, launch teasers, and narrative brand pieces  
6. **Authorized identity continuity** — Identity Preservation for approved talent/brand subjects  

## Positioning by customer segment

### Creators

**Promise:** Turn prompts and stills into usable video for clients and audiences without owning a studio.  
**Entry:** Tester ($5) → Standard ($89/mo) as output becomes habitual.  
**Message:** Professional AI video, clear seconds budget, trust-first product.

### Agencies

**Promise:** Compress creative production cycles; deliver more concepts and cuts per retainer.  
**Fit:** Digital, creative, and performance agencies with recurring video demand.  
**Message:** Studio workflow + credit predictability for client work; compliance narrative that survives client brand review.

### Marketing companies

**Promise:** Own more of the video supply chain in-house—ads, launches, always-on content.  
**Operator affinity:** RTAS Digital Marketing Company understands this buyer’s economics and campaign pressure.  
**Message:** From brief to motion faster, with published pricing and live legal/trust pages.

### Media houses

**Promise:** Hybrid pipelines—AI for ideation, previz, and selective finished assets alongside human craft.  
**Caveat:** Expect evaluation on quality consistency, rights, and operational controls; RTAS is earlier-stage than category giants.  
**Message:** Serious commercial packaging and policy clarity for pilot programs.

### Studios (production / post)

**Promise:** Capacity overflow and look-development acceleration.  
**Message:** Supplement crews; do not market as “fire the crew.”

### Business customers (SMBs & startups)

**Promise:** Launch-quality video without six-figure production budgets.  
**Message:** Standard and Premium tiers map to growth and brand-sensitive spend.

### Enterprise

**Promise (aspirational, honest):** A governed AI video capability for marketing orgs that require policy clarity, MoR professionalism, and eventual volume commercial terms.  
**Current reality:** Product and GTM are earlier on the enterprise maturity curve than Runway/Synthesia-class vendors. Enterprise is a **roadmap segment**, not a claimed installed base.  
**Message:** Trust & Safety, AI Policy, Refund, and Paddle MoR as foundations for future enterprise procurement.
