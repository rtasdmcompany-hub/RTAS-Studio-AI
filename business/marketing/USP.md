## Primary USP (one statement)

**RTAS Studio AI is the AI video studio for commercials, music videos, and animation—with transparent seconds-based credits and a trust-first posture (authorized Identity Preservation, live Trust & Safety, Paddle MoR)—built for creators, agencies, and marketing companies who need production outcomes, not deepfake toys.**

## USP pillars

### 1. Studio outcomes, not novelty clips

Positioned around **commercials, music videos, animation**, and brand storytelling via text/image-to-video—matching how marketing and creative teams actually brief work.

### 2. Transparent credit economics

**1 credit = 1 second.** Published tiers (**VERIFIED**):

- Tester — **$5** / 30 seconds / 5 days  
- Standard — **$89/mo** / 2000 seconds  
- Premium 4K — **$249/mo** / 2000 seconds  

Buyers can estimate cost per deliverable without opaque “contact sales” as the only path.

### 3. Trust-first generative video

- **No** face-swap / deepfake marketing  
- **Identity Preservation** framed for **authorized** content only  
- Live **Terms, Privacy, Refund, Cookies, AI Policy, Trust & Safety v1.1**  
- **Paddle** Merchant of Record path (domain approval dependent)  

This is a deliberate wedge as platforms and payment partners harden AUP enforcement.

### 4. Built by a marketing operator

Operated by **RTAS Digital Marketing Company** within the **RTAS brand ecosystem**—empathy for agency margins, campaign deadlines, and client brand review—not only research demo culture.

### 5. Accessible ramp

Low-friction **Tester** entry into serious **Standard** and **Premium 4K** studio tiers—designed for creator-to-agency expansion.
