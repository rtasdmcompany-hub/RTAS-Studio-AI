# RTAS Studio AI — Mission, Vision & Core Values

**Phase:** 13 · Sprint 1  
**Product:** RTAS Studio AI · https://rtasstudio.com  
**Operator:** RTAS Digital Marketing Company (Pakistan operating base) · RTAS brand ecosystem  
**Integrity:** Aspirational statements below do not invent current traction, awards, or market rank.

---

## Mission

Help creators, agencies, and marketing teams produce professional AI video — commercials, music videos, and stories — with **Authorized Identity Preservation**, transparent second-based credits, and a trust-first studio experience they can put in front of clients and audiences.

---

## Vision

Become a recognized international AI video studio brand that sets the standard for **authorized, commercially packaged generative video** — where identity consistency, clear economics, and responsible use are inseparable from creative power.

*(Vision = long-term direction. Not a claim of current category leadership.)*

---

## Positioning one-liner (bridge)

The international AI video studio for authorized identity consistency — priced by the second.

---

## Core values

| Value | Meaning in practice | Anti-pattern |
|-------|---------------------|--------------|
| **Authorized creativity** | Identity Preservation only for likenesses the user owns or is cleared to use | Face-swap / deepfake marketing |
| **Transparent economics** | 1 credit = 1 second; published Tester / Standard / Premium | Opaque token math and bait pricing |
| **Craft over gimmick** | Studio outcomes for ads, music, and stories | Novelty clip hype as the brand |
| **Trust-first packaging** | Live policies (Terms, Privacy, AI Policy, Trust & Safety) readable by client legal | “Move fast, invent compliance later” |
| **Operator honesty** | Say what is shipped; label roadmap and hypotheses | Fake logos, SLA theater, invented rankings |
| **International ambition** | English-first SaaS for global digital buyers; Pakistan base stated plainly | Pretending geography or inventing local market dominance |
