## Primary USP (one sentence)

**RTAS Studio AI is the international AI video studio that combines multi-format professional generation (music videos, commercials, animation, and stories) with Authorized Identity Preservation and transparent second-based credits — backed by a live legal policy suite and a commercial studio path from Tester to Premium 4K.**

## Investor-ready USP section

### What we sell

A cloud AI video studio SaaS for commercial creative outcomes, with published second-based pricing and a trust-first authorized-identity posture.

### Why we can win a wedge (hypothesis + proof)

| Element | Type |
|---------|------|
| Clear $5 → $89 → $249 land-and-expand ladder | Verified packaging |
| Authorized Identity Preservation as anti-deepfake commercial posture | Verified positioning + policies |
| Marketing-operator packaging for agencies and in-house teams | Operator fact + GTM hypothesis |
| Category growth in generative video | Market hypothesis |
| Traction / ARR / logos | **Only when verified** — none invented here |

### What we refuse to claim to investors

- Model R&D parity with Google Veo, Kling, or Runway  
- Fabricated MAU, ARR, NPS, or win rates  
- Fortune deployment counts or awards that do not exist  
- Payment status beyond what is operationally true (MoR historically Paddle; may be migrating)

### Investor elevator (~30s)

Early-stage AI video studio SaaS from RTAS Digital Marketing Company (Pakistan). We package commercial generation with authorized identity consistency and second-based credits — Tester $5, Standard $89/mo, Premium 4K $249/mo. We differentiate on trust posture and studio packaging for marketing buyers, not on proprietary frontier-model ownership or undisclosed traction.
