## Competitor narratives

### Runway

**They win on:** Quality perception, workflow suite, filmmaker brand, capital.  
**RTAS wedge:** Clear Tester economics; commercial packaging; authorized-identity / AUP story.  
**Don’t say:** We match their suite maturity or frame quality.

### Pika

**They win on:** Creator mindshare, approachable generation, social-native culture.  
**RTAS wedge:** Ads / music-video studio framing + trust pages for agency buyers.  
**Don’t say:** Larger consumer brand than Pika today.

### Kling

**They win on:** High-visibility demos; perceived realism/length narratives.  
**RTAS wedge:** Packaged SaaS studio, credit transparency, Western marketing ICP focus, trust posture.  
**Don’t say:** Demo supremacy over Kling.

### Veo (Google)

**They win on:** Frontier research quality, infrastructure, distribution potential.  
**RTAS wedge:** Independent commercial studio product, published plans, operator accountability.  
**Don’t say:** Model parity with Veo.

### Luma

**They win on:** Design-led creative gen brand; rapid iteration culture.  
**RTAS wedge:** Credit transparency for marketing buyers; Authorized Identity Preservation emphasis.  
**Don’t say:** Automatic quality lead over Luma.

### HeyGen

**They win on:** Avatar scale, localization, marketing video automation.  
**RTAS wedge:** Non-avatar-first studio generation for commercials and music-driven work.  
**Don’t say:** We replace their avatar enterprise stack.
