**Phase:** 13 · Sprint 1  
**Product:** RTAS Studio AI · https://rtasstudio.com  
**Pricing (verified):** Tester $5 / 30s / 5 days · Standard $89/mo / 2000s · Premium $249/mo / 2000s · **1 credit = 1 second**  
**Spend bands:** Estimates for planning — not claimed historical ARPU.  
**Aligned prior art:** `business/sales/ICP.md` (extended here with SaaS Company ICP).

---

## ICP summary

**Best-fit customer:** An English-speaking creator, agency, marketing team, startup, or SaaS company that needs recurring short-to-mid-form video (ads, social, music video, product storytelling), will pay for predictable monthly seconds, and prefers **Authorized Identity Preservation** over deepfake-adjacent tools.

**Disqualify quickly:** Unauthorized likeness seekers · free unlimited commercial output · day-one SOC2/SSO/MSA without a pilot mindset · buyers demanding verified frontier-model parity with Veo/Kling/Runway.
