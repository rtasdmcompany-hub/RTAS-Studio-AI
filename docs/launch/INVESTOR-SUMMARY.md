# Investor summary

## Problem

Creators and marketing teams need cinematic AI video without building their own GPU stack, billing compliance, or studio UX.

## Solution

RTAS Studio AI — guided Studio + Dashboard + MoR billing + cloud AI pipeline, operated as a proper SaaS.

## Traction readiness

- Production web app live on Vercel
- Auth, credits, checkout, webhooks, library, help, feedback
- Enterprise security and ops documentation

## Business model

Subscription plans (Tester / Standard / Premium) with monthly credit pools; MoR handles tax and invoicing.

## Moat

Integrated product surface (not a raw API), credit economics, identity-aware generation, and enterprise ops posture.

## Use of funds (illustrative)

GPU capacity, MoR/live payment ops, Customer Success content, brand domain, and growth.
