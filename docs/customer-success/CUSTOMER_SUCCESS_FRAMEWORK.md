# Customer Success Framework

**Product:** RTAS Studio AI · https://rtasstudio.com  
**Operator:** RTAS Digital Marketing Company  
**Phase:** 13 · Sprint 6  
**Integrity:** No fake users, tickets, CSAT/NPS, or revenue. Empty states are valid.

---

## Mission

Help paying customers achieve usable commercial video outcomes within their credit budget, renew confidently, and expand — without inventing white-glove SLAs the team cannot staff.

## Success by tier

| Tier | Price | First-cycle success |
|------|-------|---------------------|
| Tester | $5 · 30s · 5 days | ≥1 successful generation; understands 1 credit = 1 second; knows upgrade path |
| Standard | $89/mo · 2000s | Recurring outputs; intentional credit burn; renews |
| Premium 4K | $249/mo · 2000s | Client-ready 4K quality; renews |

## Lifecycle

1. **Welcome** — verify email, Dashboard, Studio  
2. **First value** — first completed render  
3. **Habit** — second project within 14 days  
4. **Renewal risk** — Tester day 3–5; Standard day 20–30 (rule-based health signals)  
5. **Expansion** — Premium or seats when volume demands  
6. **Save / exit** — tickets + churn recommendations + honest feedback

## Operating rules

- Tickets and surveys exist only after real submissions.  
- Health risk is heuristic, not ML.  
- Identity Preservation remains authorized-use only.
