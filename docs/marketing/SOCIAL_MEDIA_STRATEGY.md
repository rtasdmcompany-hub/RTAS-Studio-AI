## 3. Messaging rules

1. Canonical plans: **Tester / Standard / Premium 4K** (aliases OK if mapped).  
2. No free trial claims — entry is Tester $5.  
3. Identity Preservation = authorized only; never “swap any face.”  
4. No fake testimonials or inflated social proof counters on site or posts.  
5. Disclose paid partnerships when they exist.

## 5. Content pillars (social)

1. **Craft** — before/after-style demos from owned Showcase assets.  
2. **Credits clarity** — 1 credit = 1 second explainers.  
3. **Trust** — authorized likeness, policies.  
4. **Shipping** — changelog and reliability notes.  
5. **Operator story** — RTAS Digital Marketing Company building for creators (honest geography).

---

## 6. Community & Reddit guardrails

- Discord: enforce Trust & Safety norms; escalate billing to email.  
- Reddit: contribute answers; never brigades, vote manipulation, or sockpuppet reviews.  
- Do not claim Discord member counts publicly unless measured and current.

---

## 7. Paid social

- Only after checkout green.  
- Creative must show real product UI/output.  
- Landing page = Pricing or homepage with Tester clarity.  
- Track CAC with real spend ÷ paying customers — leave blank until data exists.

---

## 8. Crisis / AUP

If content is accused of deepfake misuse: point to `/ai-policy` and `/trust-safety`, pause amplifying risky creative, route legal to `legal@rtasstudio.com`.
