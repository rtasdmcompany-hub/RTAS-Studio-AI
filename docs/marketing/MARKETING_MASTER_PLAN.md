## 1. Mission

Build durable demand for RTAS Studio AI so creators, agencies, and marketing teams:

1. Discover the product honestly (SEO, content, social, outreach).  
2. Start with **Tester** (pay-to-prove — not free-credit tourism).  
3. Convert to **Standard** or **Premium 4K** when output quality and credit economics fit.  
4. Renew, expand seats/usage, and (later) enter enterprise / partner motions without inventing traction.

## 3. Brand awareness

**Goals (qualitative):** Be findable as an international AI video studio for commercials, music videos, and animation; be known for transparent credits and authorized Identity Preservation.

**Levers:**

- Consistent master narrative from [`MESSAGING_FRAMEWORK.md`](../branding/MESSAGING_FRAMEWORK.md).  
- Showcase loops + How-to-use as proof of craft (real product media only).  
- Founder-led LinkedIn + YouTube explainers (no fake follower programs).  
- PR only when there is a real news hook — see branding PR plan; do not invent press.

**Guardrails:** No “#1 AI video” claims. No fabricated customer logos. No deepfake positioning.

---

## 4. Acquisition

**Primary funnel:** Visit → Signup → Tester checkout → First successful render → Standard/Premium.

**Hard gate:** Do not scale paid media until Paddle checkout is production-stable and collections are measurable.

**Channel stack (priority):**

1. Organic SEO + content (always-on).  
2. Founder outbound to agencies / marketing companies.  
3. Organic social + Discord community.  
4. Paid search / social (after MoR green + CAC measurement).  
5. Affiliate / referral (Proposed until payout + legal ready).  
6. Enterprise outreach (parallel, low volume, high touch).

Details: [`CUSTOMER_ACQUISITION_CHANNELS.md`](CUSTOMER_ACQUISITION_CHANNELS.md).
