## 1. Content goals

1. Teach buyers how to produce commercials, music videos, and animation in Studio.  
2. Encode trust: authorized Identity Preservation, AUP alignment, transparent credits (1 credit = 1 second).

## 4. Channel playbooks

### Blog
- Extend `/blog` from link-hub toward real articles (or MDX) on priority topics below.  
- Every post: clear H1, canonical plan names, CTA to Pricing or How-to-use.  
- No fabricated customer stories.

### Tutorials
- Mirror and deepen `/how-to-use` and Help troubleshooting.  
- Screen recordings on YouTube; clip for TikTok/IG/Reels.

### Case studies
- **Only** with written customer permission and real metrics from that customer.  
- Until then: use Showcase + workflow narratives labeled as product demos, not customer ROI.

### Docs
- Keep `/docs`, Developers, and Help aligned with billing truth.  
- Changelog as product content feed.

### Newsletters
- See [`EMAIL_MARKETING_SYSTEM.md`](EMAIL_MARKETING_SYSTEM.md) — welcome, activation, education, updates.

### AI education
- Explain seconds-based pricing, authorized likeness, segment + stitch for long video — demystify without fearmongering competitors.

### Industry reports
- Deferred until sample size and methodology exist. Do not publish vanity “State of AI Video” with invented data.

### Video
- YouTube: studio walkthroughs, plan explainer, Identity Preservation (authorized).  
- Shorts: 15–30s craft clips from Showcase (rights-cleared assets only).

### Community
- Discord weekly craft prompt; Reddit: answer honestly, no spam seeding; no bought upvotes.

## 7. Production standards

- Voice: craft over hype; commercial clarity.  
- Always use canonical plan names; aliases secondary.  
- Cite live URLs on rtasstudio.com.  
- No stock “happy customer” quotes without consent.  
- SEO: one primary intent per page; internal links to Pricing, Features, How-to-use, Trust pages.
