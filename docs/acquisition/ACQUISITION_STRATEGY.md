## 2. Strategic principle

**Build a durable SaaS first. Treat acquisition as an optionality outcome, not a 2026 KPI.**

Acquisition conversations become credible when three conditions hold as **facts** (not narratives):

1. **Commercial engine:** Paddle Merchant of Record checkout can complete for Tester / Standard / Premium on https://rtasstudio.com (today: **honest gap** if domain/checkout activation remains pending).  
2. **Trust package:** Legal suite **v1.1 APPROVED** remains live and marketing stays Identity Preservation / authorized-content only (already **verified**).  
3. **Diligence hygiene:** Corporate, IP chain-of-title, and metrics can be produced without invention (today: **partial** — see Sprint 3 checklist).

Until those gates clear, preferred use of this strategy is **internal readiness** and **selective strategic partnership cultivation**—not a marketed “for sale” process.

## 5. Value thesis for buyers (honest)

Buyers should evaluate RTAS Studio AI primarily as:

1. **Time-to-product:** End-to-end Studio → credits → generation → library SaaS, not a raw model demo.  
2. **Commercial packaging:** Verified tiers — Tester **$5** / 30s / 5 days; Standard **$89/mo** / 2,000s; Premium **$249/mo** / 2,000s; **1 credit = 1 second**.  
3. **Compliance posture:** Public Terms, Privacy, Refund, Cookies, AI Policy, Trust & Safety v1.1; anti–deepfake / authorized Identity Preservation framing aligned with MoR AUP expectations.  
4. **Operating leverage:** Pakistan-based operator cost structure with English-first global distribution.  
5. **Adjacency:** RTAS Digital Marketing Company DNA for agency/marketing workflows (distribution synergy is **optional**, not a booked pipeline).

**Do not sell:** Exclusive foundation-model IP, patented algorithms, invented ARR multiples, or “strategic interest from [named company].”

Methodological valuation framing remains in [`Company-Valuation.md`](../business/Company-Valuation.md)—**not an ask price**.

### 6.2 Outreach posture

- **Outbound:** Only after Phase 0 gates; use category profiles in [`IDEAL_ACQUIRER_PROFILES.md`](./IDEAL_ACQUIRER_PROFILES.md)—**categories, not claimed interest**.  
- **Parallel fundraising:** Allowed if stories stay consistent (stage honesty, MoR gap). Prefer one primary narrative per conversation; see Sprint 5 fundraising strategy.

1. Public site + legal pages + pricing  
2. NDA  
3. Executive buyer brief ([`EXECUTIVE_SUMMARY_FOR_BUYERS.md`](./EXECUTIVE_SUMMARY_FOR_BUYERS.md))

5. Architecture / security pointers (repo docs)  
6. Financials / corporate / IP (secured VDR only)  
7. Source access / secrets — **only late diligence under counsel rules**

Never place live credentials, PANs, or production secrets in git.

## 7. Deal structure preferences (negotiation guide — not terms sheet)

| Topic | Preferred posture (PLANNING) | Red flags |
|-------|------------------------------|-----------|
| Structure | Clarity on asset vs equity; clean assignment of product IP and domain | Ambiguous ownership between personal and company assets |
| Price | Anchor on diligence-grade metrics + replacement-cost floor when ARR thin | Buyer demands invented historicals |
| Earn-out | Accept only if metrics are measurable and MoR-controlled | Earn-out on vanity MAU or unowned model quality SLAs |
| Retention | Founder/key eng transition plan with realistic duration | Indefinite non-competes without consideration |
| Brand | Preserve or sunset plan explicit | Forced deepfake repositioning |
| Employees / contractors | Continuity plan + assignment of work product | Undocumented contractor IP |
| Customers | Transfer notices per Terms / Privacy | Surprise changes to paid entitlements |

**Counsel-owned:** Actual SPA/APA, tax, employment, and Pakistan entity transfer mechanics.

## 9. Risks this strategy actively manages

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|------------|
| MoR / checkout incomplete | Disclose early; pause “financial buyer” outreach; continue build-vs-buy talks only with eyes open |
| Key-person concentration | Runbooks, access matrix, second demo owner (founder action) |
| Model-provider dependency (e.g. Fal) | Document in tech DD; no claim of owned weights |
| Category competition | Position on packaging + trust, not model supremacy |
| Narrative drift | Keep Identity Preservation / AUP discipline; legal v1.1 as floor |
| Premature process | Stay in Phase 0–1 until corporate/IP pack exists |
