## 1. Partnership philosophy

**Partnerships exist to create distribution, trust, or product leverage that RTAS cannot buy cheaply alone.**

Rules:

1. **No fake logos.** Do not publish partner marks without written permission.  
2. **MoR honesty.** If Paddle checkout is pending, do not promise partners “frictionless global checkout.”  
3. **AUP discipline.** Partners must not market RTAS for unauthorized deepfake / face-swap uses.  
4. **SaaS first.** Services from RTAS Digital Marketing Company may accompany deals; they do not replace product packaging.  
5. **Optionality, not obligation.** Partnerships may later support strategic investment or acquisition talks; that path is never the 2026 primary KPI.

## 3. Qualification criteria

### Must-have (all types)

| Criterion | Check |
|-----------|-------|
| Audience overlap with ICP (creators, agencies, marketers, media) | Required |
| Willingness to respect Identity Preservation / Trust & Safety | Required |
| Clear commercial owner on both sides | Required |
| Ability to measure outcomes (even simply: referred checkouts) | Required once MoR live |
| Written agreement before public claims | Required |

### Nice-to-have

- Existing creative/martech footprint  
- Regional reach (South Asia, MENA, English-global) complementary to RTAS  
- Security maturity if enterprise cosell  

### Automatic declines

- Partners pushing unauthorized likeness misuse  
- Partners requiring fabricated case studies  
- Exclusive lock-ups that block MoR or core pricing without board approval

## 5. Partnership lifecycle

```
Identify → Qualify → NDA (if needed) → Pilot SOW → Measure →
  ├─ Renew / expand commercial terms
  ├─ Pause / terminate
  └─ (Optional, multi-year) Strategic investment or acquisition dialogue
```

**Acquisition path note:** A partnership becomes an acquisition conversation only when both parties explicitly escalate under [`ACQUISITION_STRATEGY.md`](./ACQUISITION_STRATEGY.md). Partnership success alone is **not** an LOI.
