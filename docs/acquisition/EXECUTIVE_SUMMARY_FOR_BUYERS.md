1. **Finished SaaS loop** — Not a raw model demo: generation gated by credits, entitlements, and library workflows.  
2. **Clear packaging** — Published tiers and second-based economics simplify attach to creator and agency motions.  
3. **Trust posture** — Identity Preservation framed for **authorized** content; public Trust & Safety and AI Policy; anti–deepfake marketing discipline aligned with MoR AUP expectations.  
4. **Time-to-product** — Rebuild cost of studio + billing + compliance packaging is material (see methodological replacement-cost discussion in Sprint 3 valuation memo—**not an ask**).  
5. **Operator adjacency** — Marketing-company DNA under the RTAS ecosystem can support agency distribution theses (optional; not booked pipeline).
