## 3. Proposed solution

**RTAS Studio AI** — an AI video studio for text/image-driven generation oriented to commercials, music videos, animation, and brand storytelling.

**Included in pilot scope**

- Access to RTAS Studio AI at https://rtasstudio.com  
- Plan seats as specified in Commercial terms  
- Seconds-based credits (**1 credit = 1 second**)  
- Onboarding session(s) as specified  
- Support via support@rtasstudio.com  
- Policy alignment under Terms, Privacy, Refund, Cookies, AI Policy, and Trust & Safety  

**Identity Preservation:** Available for **authorized** likenesses and media the customer owns or has rights to use. RTAS does **not** market or support unauthorized deepfake or face-swap misuse.

**Explicitly out of scope (unless separately agreed in writing)**

- On-premises / air-gapped deployment  
- Custom model training on customer proprietary weights  
- Guaranteed Hollywood-equivalent output on every generation  
- SSO / SCIM / custom MSA SLA packaging beyond what is currently published  
- Any workflow involving unauthorized third-party likeness
