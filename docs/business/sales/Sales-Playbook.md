## 1. Sales philosophy

RTAS Studio AI sells **production outcomes**, not novelty. Every conversation must reinforce:

1. **Studio categories** — commercials, music videos, animation, brand storytelling  
2. **Transparent economics** — 1 credit = 1 second; Tester $5 / 30s / 5 days; Standard $89/mo / 2000s; Premium 4K $249/mo / 2000s  
3. **Trust-first posture** — authorized Identity Preservation only; no deepfake or face-swap marketing; legal suite v1.1 live  
4. **Honest stage** — early commercial SaaS with strong policy foundations; enterprise is a design-partner motion, not a claimed Fortune installed base  
5. **Operator empathy** — RTAS Digital Marketing Company understands agency margins, campaign deadlines, and client brand review  

**Execution gap (always disclose internally):** Paddle is Merchant of Record; checkout / domain review may still be pending. Do not promise “payments live globally” until status is confirmed. Self-serve conversion and enterprise pilots both depend on a working checkout path.

**Non-negotiables**

| Do | Do not |
|----|--------|
| Qualify for authorized-use Intent | Market unauthorized likeness tools |
| Lead with Tester → Standard ladder | Invent ARR, MAU, or customer logos |
| Use published tiers as the price truth | Discount below sustainable unit economics without founder approval |
| Escalate legal/security questions to documented policies | Over-promise SSO, SLA, or SOC 2 before they exist |
| Log every opportunity in CRM stages | Run “shadow deals” off Slack DMs with no forecast |

## 2. Funnel overview

```text
Aware → Interested → Evaluated → Paid trial/plan → Retained → Expanded → (Enterprise) Renewed
```

| Stage | Primary motion | Success signal |
|-------|----------------|----------------|
| Aware | SEO, content, LinkedIn, partner mentions, founder outreach | Visit to rtasstudio.com / inbound email |
| Interested | Pricing clarity, ICP fit message, trust pages | Signup intent / reply / demo request |
| Evaluated | Tester ($5) or guided demo | Generation completed; positive quality feedback |
| Paid | Standard or Premium checkout (Paddle) | Active subscription |
| Retained | Support, usage coaching, credit planning | Month-2 renewal / continued spend |
| Expanded | Multi-seat, Premium upgrade, agency pods | Seat count or ARPU up |
| Enterprise renewed | MSA/pilot close → expansion → renewal | Contract signature + renewal date |

**Self-serve default:** Creators, freelancers, startups, and small agencies.  
**Assisted default:** Agencies ≥10 people, marketing companies, media houses, and enterprise pilots.

## 3. Inbound sales motion

### 3.1 Sources

- Website (pricing, studio, help, contact)  
- Email: contact@ · info@ · support@ @rtasstudio.com  
- Organic / SEO and help-center discovery  
- Referral from RTAS Digital Marketing Company network  
- Content that surfaces commercial use cases (not deepfake tropes)

### 3.2 Inbound SLA

| Trigger | Owner | Response target |
|---------|-------|-----------------|
| Demo / enterprise inquiry | Founder or designated AE | ≤4 business hours (same calendar day preferred) |
| Pricing / plan clarification | Sales or support | ≤8 business hours |
| Technical generation issue | support@ | Per published support posture |
| Unauthorized-use request | Trust response | Decline + cite Trust & Safety / AI Policy; do not sell |

### 3.3 Inbound script skeleton

1. Thank + confirm use case (ad, music video, animation, social pack)  
2. Confirm authorized-media intent for any likeness / Identity Preservation  
3. Map to tier: Tester for proof; Standard for HD commercial; Premium for 4K cinematic  
4. Offer path: self-serve checkout **or** 30-minute demo if multi-seat / procurement  
5. Note MoR: Paddle processes payment; disclose any known checkout caveats honestly  
6. Book next step; log CRM stage **Lead → Qualified**

## 4. Outbound sales motion

### 4.1 Who to target (from ICP)

Priority order for 2026 outbound:

1. Digital / creative / performance **agencies** (5–80 people)  
2. **Marketing companies** with always-on video demand  
3. **Startups / growth teams** in launch or paid-acquisition mode  
4. **Production boutiques** needing previz / overflow capacity  
5. **Enterprise marketing orgs** only as design partners (pilot mindset)

Disqualify: unauthorized deepfake seekers; free-unlimited hunters; buyers requiring full enterprise stack on day one with no pilot.

### 4.2 Cadence (founder-led / lean team)

| Day | Touch | Channel |
|-----|-------|---------|
| 0 | Personalized value note (use case + credit math) | Email or LinkedIn |
| 2 | Soft bump + Tester offer | Same thread |
| 5 | Proof angle (legal/trust + studio categories) | Email |
| 9 | Demo invite (30 min) | Email / LinkedIn |
| 14 | Break-up / nurture park | Email |

**Volume discipline (planning):** Quality over spray. Aim for personalized batches of 15–25 accounts/week until a dedicated SDR exists.

### 4.3 Outbound message pillars

- Compress client video cycles without claiming Hollywood VFX replacement  
- Predictable seconds budget (2000s Standard/Premium)  
- Trust narrative that survives client brand review  
- Low-risk entry: Tester $5  

Never lead with “deepfake,” “face swap,” or celebrity impersonation.

## 5. Enterprise sales motion

Enterprise is a **developing ICP**. Sell **pilots**, not imaginary global rollouts.

| Principle | Practice |
|-----------|----------|
| Design partner first | 30–90 day Premium or multi-seat Standard pilot |
| Legal-first diligence | Point to Terms, Privacy, Refund, Cookies, AI Policy, Trust & Safety v1.1 |
| MoR professionalism | Paddle as Merchant of Record; note any checkout/domain gaps |
| Governance language | Authorized Identity Preservation; AUP alignment |

## 6. Demo standards

Use [`Enterprise-Demo-Script.md`](./Enterprise-Demo-Script.md) for the 30-minute format.

**Demo objectives**

1. Prove studio fit for the buyer’s real brief  
2. Make credit math obvious (seconds → plan)  
3. Surface trust posture before procurement asks  
4. Exit with a written next step (Tester, paid plan, or pilot SOW)

**Demo hygiene**

- Pre-load an **authorized** sample workflow only  
- Avoid unfinished renders as the hero moment  
- Have pricing table ready; no improvising discounts  
- Record objections in CRM

## 7. Discovery framework

Ask before you pitch. Capture in CRM notes.

| Area | Questions |
|------|-----------|
| Jobs | What video types must you ship monthly? Ads, MV, animation, social? |
| Volume | Rough seconds or deliverables per month? |
| Current stack | Agency freelancers, Runway/Pika/etc., in-house editors? |
| Decision | Who signs? Who blocks (legal, brand, finance)? |
| Timing | Campaign or launch date driving urgency? |
| Risk | Any likeness / talent rights constraints? |
| Success | What would make a 30-day pilot a clear win? |
| Budget | Comfortable with Standard ($89) vs Premium ($249) vs multi-seat? |

**BANT-lite score:** Budget · Authority · Need · Timing — each 0–2; ≥6 = sales-qualified.

## 8. Closing methodology

### 8.1 Self-serve close

- Confirm tier → send pricing page / checkout link  
- If Paddle path blocked: set expectation, offer waitlist / manual follow-up, never fake a charge  
- Confirm email for receipts and support@ path  

### 8.2 Assisted / enterprise close

1. Written summary of discovery outcomes  
2. Proposal (see template) with pilot scope, seats, credits, term  
3. Mutual action plan: legal review → commercial terms → kickoff  
4. Signature path: founder commercial approval; legal for any custom language  
5. Onboarding call within 5 business days of payment/contract  

**Discount policy (lean stage):** Prefer value-adds (onboarding session, priority support window) over deep price cuts. Any discount >15% off published monthly requires founder approval.

## 10. Objection handling (standard answers)

| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| “Quality won’t match our agency” | Position as acceleration + concept-to-cut; Premium for hero 4K; human finish still valid |
| “We need SSO / SOC 2 now” | Acknowledge roadmap; offer pilot under current controls + legal pack; do not invent certifications |
| “Is this for deepfakes?” | No. Authorized Identity Preservation only; cite Trust & Safety / AI Policy |
| “Too early / risky vendor” | Agree stage honesty; Tester $5 and short pilot de-risk; Paddle MoR + live legal suite |
| “Competitors are free / cheaper” | Compare commercial packaging, credit clarity, trust posture—not model R&D spend |
| “Checkout isn’t working” | Treat as execution gap; escalate MoR status; do not blame the buyer |
