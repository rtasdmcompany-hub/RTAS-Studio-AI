## 1. Objective

Build a repeatable system to acquire **ICP-fit paying customers** (Standard $89/mo and Premium $249/mo) via outbound DMs, email, LinkedIn, conferences, and partnerships—while protecting brand trust (no deepfake marketing) and respecting the **Paddle MoR / checkout execution gap**.

Secondary path: high-trust partnerships that can mature into deeper commercial alliances or, years later, **strategic acquisition conversations**—never the primary 2026 KPI.

## 3. Direct messages (DM) playbook

Channels: LinkedIn primary; Instagram/X only if ICP-dense and professional tone holds.

### 3.1 DM principles

- Personalize with a real observation (recent campaign, showreel, hiring post)  
- One value idea + one soft CTA  
- No fake “loved your post” openers without specificity  
- Never lead with deepfake / celebrity face  

### 3.2 DM script — Agency creative lead

> [Name] — noticed [specific campaign/work]. We built RTAS Studio AI (rtasstudio.com) for agencies that need more commercial/music-video-style concepts per sprint without opaque AI pricing. Credits are seconds-based (1 credit = 1s); Standard is $89/mo for 2000s. Open to a 15-min look if you’re testing AI in client workflows?

### 3.3 DM script — Startup growth lead

> [Name] — saw [launch/product]. If video creatives are a bottleneck for paid social, RTAS Studio AI lets teams trial for $5/30s then run Standard at $89/mo / 2000s. Trust-first positioning (authorized Identity Preservation only). Worth a quick compare against your current stack?

### 3.4 DM cadence

Day 0 note → Day 4 bump (new angle) → Day 10 break-up. Max 3 DMs. Move warm replies to email/calendar.

## 4. Email outreach

### 4.1 Subject lines (patterns)

- “Seconds-based AI video for [Agency]’s ad variants”  
- “Tester $5 path for [Company] creative throughput”  
- “Authorized-identity AI video studio — quick fit check?”  

Avoid: “#1 AI,” “guaranteed viral,” “deepfake tool.”

### 4.2 Email body — Agency (≈110 words)

> Hi [Name],  
>  
> RTAS Studio AI is an AI video studio for commercials, music videos, and animation—with transparent credits (1 credit = 1 second). Teams use Tester ($5 / 30 seconds / 5 days) to evaluate, then Standard ($89/mo / 2000s) or Premium 4K ($249/mo) for production.  
>  
> We’re operated by RTAS Digital Marketing Company and deliberately don’t market unauthorized deepfakes—Identity Preservation is authorized-use only, with Trust & Safety v1.1 live.  
>  
> If [Agency] is expanding AI-assisted client video, I can show a 30-minute workflow on one of your brief types.  
>  
> Open to a slot next week?  
> [Name] · https://rtasstudio.com · contact@rtasstudio.com  

### 4.3 Email cadence

| Touch | Day | Angle |
|-------|-----|-------|
| 1 | 0 | Use-case + pricing clarity |
| 2 | 3 | Trust / client brand-review angle |
| 3 | 7 | Tester offer / demo invite |
| 4 | 12 | Case-style question (“how do you budget video seconds today?”) |
| 5 | 18 | Break-up |

Stop on reply or hard no. Log all in CRM with source `outbound_email`.

### 4.4 Deliverability hygiene

- Send from @rtasstudio.com  
- Plain text or light HTML  
- No purchased spam lists  
- Respect unsubscribe  
- Disclose MoR honestly if asked about payment

## 6. Conferences and events

### 6.1 Event selection criteria

| Prefer | Avoid |
|--------|-------|
| Marketing, creator, AI-creative, agency summits | Pure deepfake/entertainment gimmick expos |
| Events with demo-friendly side rooms | Pay-to-speak vanity with no ICP density |
| Regional: Dubai, London, US coastal creator/marketing, South Asia digital | Random unrelated trade shows |

### 6.2 Event playbook

**Before:** Book 8–12 meetings; prepare Tester QR / pricing card; one-pager facts only.  
**During:** Demo authorized workflows only; capture leads same day in CRM.  
**After:** 48-hour follow-up with personalized note referencing conversation.

**Budget rule:** Events compete with paid ads—require pipeline targets (e.g., ≥10 SQLs) to justify spend.

## 7. Partnership → deeper alliance (and long-range acquisition path)

### 7.1 Partnership acquisition funnel

```text
Intro → Joint use-case fit → Design partner seats → Co-marketing
  → Referral agreement → Strategic commercial alliance
  → (Optional, multi-year) Strategic investment or acquisition dialogue
```

### 7.2 Partner outreach targets

- Mid-size agencies wanting AI production capacity  
- Marketing companies in RTAS network  
- Complementary SaaS (stock, brief, DAM-adjacent)—no capability overclaim  
- Educator/creator collectives for ethical AI video  

### 7.3 Partnership DM/email wedge

> We’re looking for [2–3] design-partner agencies to run RTAS Studio AI on real client-safe workflows—published pricing, authorized Identity Preservation, Paddle MoR. In exchange: priority onboarding + co-marketing if results are permissioned. Not a reseller lock-in.

### 7.4 When partnership becomes “acquisition path”

Only after: mutual revenue relevance, cultural/AUP fit, and strategic logic. Then route through [`Investor-Outreach-Strategy.md`](./Investor-Outreach-Strategy.md) strategic/CVC process. **Do not** pitch “acquire us” in first customer emails.

## 10. Measurement

Tie outreach to CRM and [`Sales-KPI.md`](./Sales-KPI.md):

- Reply rate by channel  
- SQL rate  
- Demo rate

- CAC proxy (time + tools + events)  
- Partner-sourced pipeline  

Review biweekly; kill low-intent sequences that attract abuse-seeking leads.
