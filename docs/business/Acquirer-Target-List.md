## Category A — AI video & generative creative platforms

**Examples (types):** Runway-class research/creative video tools · Pika/Luma-class generation apps · open-model ecosystem companies commercializing video UX.

| Strategic fit | Rationale |
|---------------|-----------|
| High product adjacency | Same end activity: generative video |
| Workflow packaging | RTAS Studio + credits + categories accelerate “commercial studio” narrative |
