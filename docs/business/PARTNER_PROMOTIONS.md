# Partner Promotions

## Policy
Partner promotions are supported by the engine but should only be activated for approved partners.

The UI must clearly distinguish partner inventory from RTAS internal promotions:
- `Partner`
- `Sponsored Partner`

## Suitable Partner Categories
- Infrastructure
- Delivery / email
- Payments
- AI model ecosystem
- Production tooling

Examples the engine is designed to support:
- Cloudflare
- Hostinger
- RunPod
- Fal AI
- Resend
- OpenRouter
- ElevenLabs
- Stripe
- Paddle

## Activation Checklist
Before marking a partner promotion active:
1. Confirm business approval
2. Confirm label wording
3. Confirm destination URL and attribution policy
4. Confirm the placement fits premium UX
5. Confirm no regulated or low-quality content risk

## Notes
- Phase 13 ships partner-ready infrastructure and labels
- Internal and educational promotions are seeded first
- Partner promotions can be activated later from `/admin/promotions`
