## 1. Business model

### What is sold
An **AI video studio SaaS**: text-to-video and image-to-video oriented to commercials, music videos, animation, and marketing workflows, with **Identity Preservation** framed for authorized content (not deepfake marketing).

### How money is collected
| Plan | Price | Allowance | Role |
|------|-------|-----------|------|
| Tester | $5 | 30 seconds · 5 days | Evaluation land |
| Standard | $89 / month | 2000 seconds | Primary commercial seat |
| Premium 4K | $249 / month | 2000 seconds | Higher-quality cinematic positioning |

**Metering:** 1 credit = 1 second.  
**Merchant of Record:** Paddle (tax/VAT/invoicing intent). **Checkout/domain approval may still be pending — material business gap.**

### Unit economic logic (conceptual)
Revenue per seat minus (inference COGS via Fal + hosting + MoR fees + support). Diligence must measure **actual seconds generated vs credits sold** and Fal wallet burn; do not assume software-like 80%+ gross margin without evidence.
