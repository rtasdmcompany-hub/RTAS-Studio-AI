# Credits & billing (product)

Customer-facing summary. Payments use a Merchant of Record (Paddle or Lemon Squeezy).

## Credits

- **1 credit = 1 second** of finished video.
- Plans grant a monthly pool that **expires** at period end.
- Early resubscribe may **roll over** remaining credits when offered in-app.

## Plans (product surface)

See `/pricing` and `docs/marketing/FEATURES.md` for current packaging.

| Entitlement | Free / trial | Paid |
|-------------|--------------|------|
| Preview | Yes (evaluation) | Yes |
| Downloadable master | No | Yes |
| Commercial license | No | Yes (active subscription) |

## Where users manage billing

- Dashboard → **Manage plans**
- Help → [Billing](/help/billing)
- Support: contact@rtasstudio.com

## Security note

Card data never touches RTAS servers. Webhooks are signature-verified and fail closed without secrets.
