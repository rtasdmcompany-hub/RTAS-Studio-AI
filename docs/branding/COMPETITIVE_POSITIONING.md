# RTAS Studio AI — Competitive Positioning

**Classification:** Brand / competitive strategy  
**Product:** RTAS Studio AI · https://rtasstudio.com  
**Integrity:** Fair and honest. No unsupported “#1,” SOTA, or win-rate claims. Qualitative judgments are planning aids—not lab benchmarks. Detailed matrix: [`business/marketing/COMPETITOR_MATRIX.md`](../../business/marketing/COMPETITOR_MATRIX.md).

---

## 1. Category definition

RTAS Studio AI competes in **generative AI video / AI video studio** for commercial creative outcomes (ads, music videos, animation, brand films), with a **seconds-based SaaS credit model**.

It does **not** primarily compete as:

- An enterprise avatar presenter suite (Synthesia / HeyGen core)  
- A frontier foundation-model lab (Google Veo-class research)  
- A deepfake / face-swap consumer toy  

---

## 2. Positioning statement (internal)

For creators, agencies, and marketing teams who need commercial video faster than traditional production, **RTAS Studio AI** is the **trust-forward AI video studio** that sells **clear seconds credits** and **authorized Identity Preservation**—unlike novelty clip apps that ignore AUP risk, and unlike avatar platforms optimized for talking-head L&D.

## 4. Differentiation pillars (usable in sales)

1. **Studio outcomes language** — commercials, music videos, animation—not only “AI clips.”  
2. **Transparent economics** — 1 credit = 1 second; Tester $5 / Standard $89 / Premium $249.  
3. **Trust-first generative video** — no deepfake/face-swap marketing; authorized Identity Preservation; live Trust & Safety / AI Policy.  
4. **MoR professionalism** — Paddle path for international checkout (note execution gaps honestly).  
5. **Operator empathy** — built inside a digital marketing company that feels campaign pressure.

## 6. Battlecards (short)

### vs creative gen leaders (Runway / Pika / Luma / Kling)

**Say:** “We’re earlier-stage on brand gravity. Buy us for credit clarity, commercial packaging, and a trust story your client legal team can read.”  
**Don’t say:** “We outperform them on every frame.”

### vs avatar leaders (Synthesia / HeyGen)

**Say:** “Different job-to-be-done—open studio generation for ads and music-driven work, not presenter templates.”  
**Don’t say:** “We replace their enterprise avatar stack.”

### vs Big Tech models (Veo et al.)

**Say:** “They set research bars. We sell a commercial studio product with published plans and support accountability.”  
**Don’t say:** “Same model quality.”

### vs DIY API stacks

**Say:** “If you want to assemble pipelines, infra tools fit. If you want a studio UX, credits, and policies, that’s RTAS.”  
**Don’t say:** “APIs are useless.”
