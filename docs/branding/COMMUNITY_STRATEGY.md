# RTAS Studio AI — Community Strategy

**Classification:** Community / brand  
**Product:** RTAS Studio AI · https://rtasstudio.com  
**Primary hub (current):** Discord — https://discord.gg/rtas  
**Integrity:** Channels listed are **current**. No member counts, “largest AI Discord,” or fabricated testimonials.

---

## 1. Community purpose

Build a trustworthy home for creators and early teams using RTAS Studio AI: share craft, get help, surface product feedback, and reinforce **authorized creativity** norms. Community is a retention and learning loop—not a vanity metric.

## 4. Community principles

1. **Authorize first** — No sharing of non-consensual likeness content; Identity Preservation norms apply socially too.  
2. **Help over hype** — Prefer reproducible tips to engagement bait.  
3. **Honest product talk** — Bugs and limits welcome; no fake “everything is perfect.”  
4. **Credit clarity** — When discussing cost, use published tiers.  
5. **Respect operators** — Harassment, spam, and scam links removed.

---

## 5. Moderation model (founder-stage)

| Role | Responsibility |
|------|----------------|
| Founder / ops | Final policy; bans; partner/press escalation |
| Trusted members (**future**) | Triage FAQ; escalate abuse |
| Auto tools | Basic Discord automod when configured |

**Escalation:** Safety/legal issues → legal@rtasstudio.com · product abuse patterns → Trust & Safety page + support@rtasstudio.com

Publish a short Discord rules post pinned: authorized use, no deepfake requests, no piracy, no fake giveaways.

---

## 6. Engagement loops

| Loop | Mechanic | KPI to track later |
|------|----------|-------------------|
| Onboarding | Welcome + link to How to Use + Tester CTA | % new members who post once |
| Craft | Weekly theme (commercial / MV / animation) | Showcase posts |
| Support | Tag help; point to /help and status | Time-to-first-response |
| Feedback | Structured feature requests | Issues shipped from community |
| Recognition | Repost great authorized work (permission) | Creator retention |

Do not buy fake members or bots.
