# RTAS Studio AI — Public Relations Plan

**Classification:** Communications / PR  
**Product:** RTAS Studio AI · https://rtasstudio.com  
**Operator:** RTAS Digital Marketing Company  
**Phase:** 11 · Sprint 7  
**Hard rule:** No fabricated press coverage, fake journalists, invented awards, or manufactured “as seen in” logos.

---

## 1. PR objective (current stage)

Earn credible awareness for an **early-stage commercial AI video studio** that differentiates on **authorized creativity**, **transparent credits**, and **trust packaging**—not on invented traction.

**Success (near-term):** Accurate mentions, founder interviews earned on real milestones, inbound from creators/agencies who understand the anti-deepfake stance.  
**Non-goals:** Fake Wired/TechCrunch badges · pay-to-play “top 10 AI tools” lists presented as editorial · deepfake controversy bait.

## 3. Newsworthy milestones (pitch only when true)

Pitch when **at least one** is verifiable:

1. Reliable public checkout / MoR path fully working for target markets.  
2. Meaningful product release (e.g., documented Premium 4K workflow improvements, Identity Preservation UX clarity).  
3. Signed partnership (named, approved).  
4. Verified customer story with written permission.  
5. Policy leadership moment (substantive Trust & Safety update worth explaining).  
6. Fundraising close (only if actually closed and disclosable).

**Do not pitch** “we launched” repeatedly without a new angle.

## 7. Process

1. **Gate:** Confirm milestone is real; legal/AUP language reviewed.  
2. **Brief:** One-page angle + boilerplate + 3 proof links.  
3. **Pitch:** Personalized email; no spray-and-pray with false urgency.  
4. **Interview prep:** Objection card from messaging framework; never speculate metrics.  
5. **Follow-up:** Once; then nurture via owned content.  
6. **Archive:** Save published links; never fabricate screenshots of coverage.

Crisis: If misuse or deepfake association appears, respond with policy links and corrective statement; escalate via legal@rtasstudio.com.
