## Enterprise thesis

Win **design partners and pilots** first via Premium and multi-seat Standard/Premium. Convert proof into custom commercial terms later. Do not skip to “Enterprise SKU on /pricing” until billing, security questionnaire depth, and support capacity exist.

**Sales language:** “Enterprise” = procurement conversation.  
**Product SKU today:** Premium / multi-seat — not a separate live Enterprise plan.

| Attribute | Fit |
|-----------|-----|
| Buyer | Mid-market brand/marketing org or large agency holding company team |
| Job | Governed experimentation with generative video for ads/campaigns |
| Start size | 1–5 seats; Premium or Standard mix |

## Procurement narrative (approved claims only)

**Say:**

- AI video studio for commercials, music videos, animation.  
- Transparent credits (1 credit = 1 second); published list pricing.  
- Trust-first: no face-swap/deepfake marketing; authorized Identity Preservation.  
- MoR: Paddle.  
- Operator: RTAS Digital Marketing Company.

**Do not say:**

- “Trusted by [invented enterprises].”  
- “SOC2 certified” unless verified true.  
- “SLA 99.99%” without a real commitment.
