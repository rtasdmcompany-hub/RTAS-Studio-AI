# RTAS Studio AI — Customer Acquisition Strategy

**Classification:** Growth / GTM  
**Product:** RTAS Studio AI · https://rtasstudio.com  
**Sprint:** Phase 11 · Sprint 8  
**Integrity:** No invented customers, influencers, or partnership deals. Channel targets are **PLANNING ASSUMPTIONS**.

---

## Objective

Acquire **paying** customers who fit the Ideal Customer Profile—creators, agencies, marketing companies, and growth teams that need recurring commercial video—without burning budget on free-credit tourism (there is **no live free plan**; free accounts have **0 credits**).

## Acquisition principle

**Pay-to-prove.** The entry product is **Tester ($5 / 30 seconds / 5 days)**, not a free credit dump. Acquisition creative and landing pages must make that honesty clear: low-risk paid proof, then Standard ($89/mo) or Premium ($249/mo).

**Hard gate:** Do not scale paid acquisition until Paddle checkout is production-stable. Pending MoR/domain approval is a **growth blocker**.

## Channel strategy

### A. Organic / SEO / content (always-on foundation)

**Job:** Capture intent around AI video for ads, music video, animation, and “seconds-based” pricing clarity.

| Tactic | Owner | Notes |
|--------|-------|-------|
| Pricing & credit clarity pages | Product marketing | Keep 1 credit = 1 second front and center |
| How-to / studio guides | Content | Use live Help / How-to-use; no fake case studies |
| Comparison honesty | Content | Cite competitor landscape without “#1” claims |
| Trust pages | Legal/ops | Terms, Privacy, Refund, AI Policy, Trust & Safety already live — link them |

**PLANNING ASSUMPTION:** Organic is the lowest-risk channel while checkout stabilizes.

### B. Founder-led outbound (primary agency motion)

**Job:** Put RTAS in front of ICP agencies and marketing companies.

| Cadence (assumption) | Action |
|----------------------|--------|
| 20 accounts / week | Short personalized notes referencing commercials / social variants / credit predictability |
| Follow-up ×2 | Demo link to https://rtasstudio.com + Tester CTA |
| Qualification | In/out checklist from ICP |

Do **not** invent case-study metrics. Offer a live product walkthrough and Tester proof.

### C. Paid acquisition (only after checkout green)

| Channel | Fit | Guardrail |
|---------|-----|-----------|
| Search (high intent) | “AI video ads”, “AI music video tool” | Cap spend until CAC known |
| Social prospecting | Creator / agency audiences | Creative must show product, not generic AI glow |
| Retargeting | Pricing / Studio visitors | Exclude unpaid tire-kickers after clear Tester CTA |

**Rule:** Pause any campaign where checkout fails or refund spikes from payment confusion.

### D. Partnerships & affiliates (proposed)

Creator educators, freelance communities, and boutique agencies as **proposed** partners—see [`AFFILIATE_PROGRAM_STRATEGY.md`](AFFILIATE_PROGRAM_STRATEGY.md). **Not live.** Do not claim partners on the site until contracted.

### E. Community & social proof (earned)

| Do | Do not |
|----|--------|
| Share real product demos and showcase assets already on site | Fabricate testimonials or logos |
| Ask early paid users for optional quotes | Promise payment for fake reviews |
| Publish changelog / help updates | Inflate user counts |

## Message architecture (acquisition)

Aligned with USP pillars:

1. **Studio outcomes** — commercials, music videos, animation (not deepfake toys).  
2. **Transparent credits** — 1 credit = 1 second; Tester $5; Standard $89; Premium $249.  
3. **Trust-first** — authorized Identity Preservation; live Trust & Safety; Paddle MoR.  
4. **Operator empathy** — built by a marketing company for marketing deadlines.

Anti-claims: no face-swap marketing, no invented ARR, no parity with Runway/Veo R&D scale.
