## Mission

Help paying customers achieve **usable commercial video outcomes** within their credit budget, renew confidently, and expand seats—without over-promising enterprise white-glove that the company cannot yet staff.

## Success definition by tier

| Tier | Price | “Success” in first cycle |
|------|-------|--------------------------|
| **Tester** | $5 · 30s · 5 days | Completes ≥1 successful generation; understands 1 credit = 1 second; knows upgrade path before day 5 |
| **Standard** | $89/mo · 2000s | Completes recurring outputs; burns credits intentionally; returns next billing cycle |
| **Premium 4K** | $249/mo · 2000s | Achieves quality bar for brand/client work; uses 4K path as intended; renews |

Free accounts (0 credits) are **not** CS-priority for “activation via free usage”—guide them to Tester.

## Lifecycle stages

| Stage | Timing | CS job |
|-------|--------|--------|
| **Welcome** | Day 0 paid | Confirm checkout, point to Studio + Help, state credit math |
| **First value** | Day 0–2 | Ensure first successful generation; unblock auth/billing issues |
| **Habit** | Day 3–14 | Encourage second project; share how-to / troubleshooting |
| **Renewal risk** | Tester day 3–5; Standard day 20–30 | Conversion / renewal nudges; usage summary |
| **Expansion** | Month 2+ | Premium upgrade or additional seats when volume/quality demands |
| **Save / exit** | Cancel signal | Diagnose; offer right-tier fit; capture honest feedback |

## Onboarding checklist (paid)

1. Account access confirmed (auth@ path if login issues).  
2. Customer knows **1 credit = 1 second**.  
3. Customer knows remaining seconds and expiry (Tester: 5 days).  
4. First project created in Studio.  
5. First generation completed or clear error resolved.  
6. Support channel known: support@rtasstudio.com.  
7. Legal/trust pointers available if client-facing work (AI Policy, Trust & Safety).

## Playbooks (brief)

### Playbook A — Tester conversion

- Day 1: Ensure first output exists.  
- Day 3: Reminder of remaining seconds + Standard value ($89 / 2000s).  
- Day 5: Expiry notice + clear checkout link.  
- Never imply free extension as policy unless ops explicitly grants case-by-case.

### Playbook B — Zero-usage Standard

- Contact within 72 hours: offer how-to link + ask blocker.  
- Common blockers: prompt skill, payment confusion, expectation mismatch.  
- If wrong tier: honest downgrade conversation beats silent churn.

### Playbook C — Quality disappointment

- Separate **model/output limits** from **billing errors**.  
- Reiterate Premium positioning if they need 4K cinematic bar.  
- Capture examples for product—do not invent roadmap dates.

### Playbook D — Brand-safety / client review

- Point to live Trust & Safety, AI Policy, authorized Identity Preservation framing.  
- Refuse assistance for unauthorized likeness use.
