## Revenue thesis

RTAS Studio AI earns revenue by selling **predictable seconds of generation**, not opaque seat theater. Buyers enter on **Tester**, convert to **Standard** as habitual producers, and upgrade to **Premium 4K** when cinematic quality justifies a higher absolute price on the same 2000-second pool.

Primary cash engine (near term): **self-serve SaaS** via Paddle. Secondary engine (build next): **founder-led agency / multi-seat** expansion. Tertiary (later): **enterprise pilots** with custom commercial terms—not claimed as installed base today.

## Revenue levers (ranked)

1. **Unblock Paddle checkout** — without reliable MoR collection, growth docs are aspirational.  
2. **Tester → Standard conversion** — primary ladder; measure within 5-day Tester window and 14/30 days after.  
3. **Standard → Premium upgrades** — quality-led ARPU; same credit pool, higher willingness to pay.  
4. **Seat expansion in agencies** — 2–5 seats before custom enterprise.  
5. **Retention / reduce involuntary churn** — failed payments, expired Tester without upgrade path.  
6. **Outbound to ICP agencies** — founder-led until sales capacity exists.  
7. **Affiliate / partner experiments** — only after checkout + refund/support SLAs are credible.
