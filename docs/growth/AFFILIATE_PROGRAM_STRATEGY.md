# RTAS Studio AI — Affiliate Program Strategy

**Classification:** Growth / partnerships  
**Product:** RTAS Studio AI · https://rtasstudio.com  
**Sprint:** Phase 11 · Sprint 8  
**Status:** **PROPOSED PROGRAM — NOT LIVE.** Do not market affiliates, commission rates, or partner logos as current fact.

## Purpose

Design a future affiliate / referral channel that sends **ICP-fit** creators and agencies to paid Tester, Standard, or Premium checkouts—after Paddle MoR is production-stable and support can absorb incremental volume.

## Why wait (gates)

| Gate | Why required |
|------|--------------|
| Paddle checkout reliable | Affiliates amplify broken payment into public failure |
| Refund / AUP clarity | Partner traffic must not incentivize abuse |
| Attribution method chosen | Need trackable links or codes via MoR/affiliate tool |
| Support capacity | Extra Testers create tickets |
| Brand guidelines ready | Partners must not invent deepfake claims ([`business/branding/README.md`](../../business/branding/README.md)) |

Until gates pass: **founder referrals only**, unpaid or case-by-case goodwill—not a public program.

| Element | Proposed design | Notes |
|---------|-----------------|-------|
| Name | RTAS Studio Partner / Affiliate | Final name TBD |

## Creative & compliance rules for partners

Partners **must**:

- Use approved product description (studio for commercials, music videos, animation).  
- State pricing truthfully: Tester $5 / 30s / 5 days; Standard $89/mo / 2000s; Premium $249/mo / 2000s; 1 credit = 1 second.  
- State there is **no free credit plan**.  
- Link Trust & Safety / AI Policy when discussing identity features.  
- Disclose affiliate relationship per FTC/local rules.

Partners **must not**:

- Market face-swap / deepfake capabilities.  
- Invent customer counts, revenue, or “official partnership with [BigCo].”  
- Promise Enterprise features that are not live.  
- Scrap or misrepresent showcase videos as partner-owned client work without labeling.

## Launch sequence (when approved)

1. Legal: short Affiliate Terms + brand rules.  
2. Economics: lock rates against measured contribution margin.  
3. Tracking: test end-to-end with one trusted partner.  
4. Soft launch: 5–10 invite-only partners.  
5. Public page on rtasstudio.com **only after** soft launch works.  
6. Kill switch: pause program if refund fraud or AUP violations spike.
