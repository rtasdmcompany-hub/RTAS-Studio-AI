## 2. Hiring principles

1. **Hire behind a bottleneck**, not ahead of vanity org charts  
2. **Support quality before paid growth**—churn is expensive  
3. Prefer contractors / fractional for specialized spikes (security review, brand design)  
4. Every role must protect **AUP / Trust & Safety** posture  
5. Remote-friendly, English-capable; operator base in Pakistan with global coverage windows
