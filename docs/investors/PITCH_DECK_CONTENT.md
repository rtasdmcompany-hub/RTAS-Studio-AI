## Slide 2 — Problem

**Video demand outruns traditional production.**

- Marketing and creator teams need continuous ads, social, music video, and brand motion  
- Studios and freelancers are slow and expensive for iteration volume  
- Generative tools are fragmented: novelty clips, avatar presenters, or research demos  
- Buyers also face **trust risk**—payment partners and enterprises reject unauthorized likeness / deepfake framing  

**Buyer job:** Ship more usable video, faster, with predictable cost and brand-safe policy.

---

## Slide 3 — Solution

**RTAS Studio AI — a production-shaped SaaS, not a raw model demo.**

- Text-to-video and image-to-video in a guided Studio  
- Credits that buyers understand: **1 credit = 1 second**  
- Dashboard, library, auth, support, and live legal/trust pages  
- **Identity Preservation** for authorized content only  
- **Paddle** Merchant of Record path for tax and invoicing  

**Live product:** https://rtasstudio.com
