## 8. Unit economics sensitivity (ASSUMPTION prompts)

Before locking allocation, founders should compute with live data:

1. **COGS per second** (Fal + overhead)  
2. **Effective revenue per second** by plan (Standard: $89 / 2000s ≈ **$0.0445/s** list; Premium: $249 / 2000s ≈ **$0.1245/s** list — VERIFIED list prices, not net of fees/refunds)  
3. **Paddle fee impact** on net revenue  
4. **Utilization** (% of purchased seconds consumed)  
5. **Support cost per paying account**  

Until (1) is measured, treat gross margin claims as **ESTIMATES only**.
