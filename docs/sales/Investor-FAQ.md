**Verified:** Production web app, documented architecture, legal **v1.1 APPROVED**, pricing constants (Tester $5 / Standard $89 / Premium $249), stack (Next.js, FastAPI, Prisma/Supabase, Fal, Resend, Vercel, Cloudflare, Paddle path).

## Why now?

1. Generative video is commercially useful for drafts, social, and many campaign assets.  
2. MoRs and enterprises reject vague deepfake positioning—RTAS already aligned public copy to **authorized content only**.  
3. Cloud GPU APIs allow capital-efficient scaling before owning silicon.

## Why AI?

AI collapses the marginal cost and latency of producing motion creative. RTAS packages that capability as **governed SaaS** (credits, auth, policies, library) so buyers can adopt it like other software vendors—not as unmanaged GPU scripts.

## Why recurring revenue?

Subscriptions (Standard $89, Premium $249) create **predictable ARPU** and encourage habitual Studio usage. Tester ($5 / 5 days / 30s) is a conversion wedge, not the core LTV engine.

Metering (**1 credit = 1 second**) aligns revenue with COGS drivers once live GPU telemetry exists.

## Future valuation — how should we think about it?

**All ranges below are Estimates / educational frameworks, not appraisals of RTAS.**

### Framework A — Revenue multiple (requires verified ARR)

\[
EV \approx ARR_{\text{verified}} \times Multiple_{\text{sector}}
\]

Illustrative sector bands often discussed for private SaaS (highly condition-dependent):

| Profile | Illustrative multiple band on ARR | Conditions |
|---------|-----------------------------------|------------|
| Early, slow growth | ~3×–8× | Weak NRR, high churn |
| Healthy growth SaaS | ~8×–15× | Solid NRR, clear GTM |
| Top-quartile AI/SaaS narrative | ~15×–25×+ | Rare; growth + margin + brand |

**RTAS application:** Do **not** apply until ARR is audited. Pre-revenue deals skew to replacement cost + strategic premium.

### Framework B — Replacement cost floor

Estimate cost for an acquirer to rebuild: Studio UX, credits/billing, legal compliance pack, MoR integration, ops docs, brand launch. Often used as a **negotiation floor**, not a ceiling.

### Framework C — DCF (illustrative)

Project 5-year cash flows with explicit assumptions (ARPU mix, churn, GPU COGS/sec, MoR fees, opex). Discount at buyer WACC. Label every year **projection**.

### Framework D — Milestone step-ups (narrative)

| Milestone | Valuation narrative effect |
|-----------|----------------------------|
| MoR live + first verified MRR | Moves from “tech asset” to “revenue asset” |
| Positive contribution margin after GPU | De-risks AI COGS story |
| Agency logos + NRR > 100% | Supports growth multiple |
| API / multi-seat | Expands SAM capture story |
